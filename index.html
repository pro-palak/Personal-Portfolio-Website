THE AUTHOR

I am a Computer Science student with a passion for designing impactful digital experiences. Whether it's coding a sleek, user-centered tool or crafting a visual masterpiece, I thrive in the intersection of creativity and technology.

I’m not buried in lines of code or immersed in design challenges, you’ll likely find me exploring the latest in music, astrophysics, or F1 (because who doesn’t love speed and precision?). I moved to the UK when I was 17, this step was one of the best decisions of my life taught me a lot about technology and life. I’ve been passionate about technology since forever and how it can be used to solve real-world problems.

With a strong foundation in Java, CSS, HTML, and a keen interest in machine learning and sustainability, I’m on a mission to craft tech solutions that are not just smart but also accessible, meaningful, and built with purpose. Let’s create something that makes an impact big or small and pushes the boundaries of what’s possible.
